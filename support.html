Documentation

Getting started

  1. Sign in. Open Nexus Auto and sign in with your Google or Apple account.
  2. Add your vehicle. Follow onboarding to enter your vehicle—use the VIN scanner, upload a photo of the VIN, type the VIN manually, or enter details manually when needed. Confirm your vehicle when everything looks correct.
  3. Explore the app. Use the bottom tabs to move between Dashboard, Records, Warranties, and About.

Dashboard

The dashboard is your home for the current vehicle: quick access to maintenance context, registration and insurance, and your account. Open registration or insurance from here when you need to review or update those details. Service record entries opened from the dashboard return you to your records flow as you navigate.

Records

The Records tab lists your service history. Add a new record to log work performed, dates, mileage, and related notes. Open any record to see full details. You can start adding a record with an optional link to a maintenance item when the app suggests it.

Warranties

Use Warranties to add and review coverage: see what is protected, important dates, and details in one place. Add new warranties as you purchase or renew coverage, and open an entry for specifics.

About

The About section provides app information and related links. Use it for version details and anything we surface about Nexus Auto on device.

Camera and photos

Nexus Auto may use the camera to scan VINs and capture maintenance documents, and may access your photo library when you import existing images. Grant permissions when prompted so these features work as intended.

Screens and exact labels may change slightly as we improve the app. If something does not match what you see on your device, use the latest version from the App Store and check release notes when available.
